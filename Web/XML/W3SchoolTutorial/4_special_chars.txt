Special chars such as <, >, etc exist in xml docs. We use alternatives such as '$lt;'

This: <message>salary < 1000</message>
Becomes this: <message>salary &lt; 1000</message>

We have the following set:
	&lt;	<	less than
	&gt;	>	greater than
	&amp;	&	ampersand 
	&apos;	'	apostrophe
	&quot;	"	quotation mark

Comments are as follows:
<!-- This is a comment -->

White spaces and new lines are also kept in XML.
