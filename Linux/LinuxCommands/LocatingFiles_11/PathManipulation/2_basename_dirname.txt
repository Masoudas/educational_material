The commands basename and dirname can be used to determine the filename or the folder path from a given path as
well.

$ path=/etc/ld/ldconfig.d
$ basename $path
ldconfig.d

$ basename -s.d $path	# Cut the extension as well.
ldconfig

$ dirname $path
/etc/ld

$ dirname $path $path
/etc/ld
/etc/ld

$ dirname stdio.h
'.'
